# Guerilla marketing plan (zero budget)

Synthesized from [`../research/16-guerilla-marketing.md`](../research/16-guerilla-marketing.md),
[`../research/18-community-building.md`](../research/18-community-building.md), and
[`../research/19-positioning-messaging.md`](../research/19-positioning-messaging.md).
Everything here is **no-cost and ethical** — no astroturf, no fake accounts, no
vote-rings. Our credibility *is* the marketing.

## The one-sentence pitch

**glamfire is the open harness for the last mile of AI: own your context, route to the
cheapest capable model, and stop renting your company brain back from a frontier lab.**

## The story spine (use everywhere)

- **Villain:** renting your own brain back — frontier labs absorbing your team's context
  until you can't leave.
- **Old way:** sign a frontier contract because the harness is too hard to build.
- **New way:** an open harness anyone can run — route the center of distribution to
  cheap open models (GLM 5.2), keep the edge on frontier, own the context.
- **Hero:** the builder. The harness-talent shortage is *your* opening.
- **Proof, not adjectives:** show the routing decision, the cost saved, the exportable
  context. Receipts over hype.

## Launch sequence (no dates — gated on reality, not calendar)

1. **Pre-launch:** README + SPEC + honest *current reality* live; demo asset recorded;
   `good first issue`s filed; Discussions on.
2. **Soft launch:** post in one niche home first (r/LocalLLaMA or Lobsters), absorb
   feedback, fix, update *current reality*.
3. **Show HN:** when `glam` install + at least one real GLM/Fireworks round-trip works
   end-to-end. Lead with the honest state. Be present all day in comments.
4. **Amplify:** the X thread + short video the same day; cross-link, don't spam.
5. **Sustain:** weekly build-in-public update tied to a real shipped mini-feature; a
   changelog; office hours; recognize every first-time contributor by name.

## The honesty moat

We publish *current reality* and never market vaporware. In a field full of demos that
don't run, "what we say works, works — here's how we verified it" is the differentiator
people remember. Every claim links to a way to reproduce it.
