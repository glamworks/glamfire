# Disclaimer

This disclaimer mirrors the non-security framing in the SEC staff's own examples and the
risk controls in [`../../research/14-crypto-legal-risk.md`](../../research/14-crypto-legal-risk.md).
**This is not legal, financial, investment, or tax advice.** Retain crypto-literate
counsel before any launch.

- **For entertainment and community/culture only.** $GLAM and $GLAMFIRE are collectible
  community tokens. They have **no use, functionality, or intrinsic value** beyond
  entertainment and social/cultural participation.
- **No expectation of profit.** You **should not expect to profit** or earn any return
  from owning these tokens. **No person intends to undertake efforts** to bring about a
  profit or increase in value for holders. They are not securities, not equity, not a
  claim on glamworks or glamfire, and confer no right to revenue, dividends, buybacks, or
  staking yield. Do not buy them expecting profit.
- **No governance, no rights.** Ownership grants **no** governance over the software, no
  votes, no access, no gated features, no premium tier, and no claim on glamworks/
  glamfire assets or income.
- **No roadmap, no promises.** There is **no roadmap, no committed deliverables, and no
  obligation** on anyone to do anything for holders. Any future activity is entirely
  discretionary and is **not owed** to holders.
- **You may lose everything.** Community tokens are extremely volatile and frequently go
  to **zero**. Only ever risk what you can afford to lose entirely.
- **The software is independent and free, and the token funds nothing.** glamfire is
  Apache-2.0 and fully usable with **no token, wallet, or blockchain**. Token proceeds
  **do not fund** software development (that is funded separately via sponsorships,
  grants, and commercial licenses). The tokens are never required to use, contribute to,
  or govern the software.
- **No price talk, no pre-sales, advertise only when live.** glamworks does not, and will
  not, tout or predict price, run pre-sales/pre-orders, promise listings, or hype the
  tokens before they are actually live and freely tradable. Tokens are announced as what
  they **are** (community/entertainment), never as an investment opportunity.
- **Scam warning.** The only legitimate token mint addresses are those published in
  [`STATUS.md`](STATUS.md) once status is **LIVE**, announced from official glamworks
  channels. Treat every other address, "presale," or "airdrop" as a scam.
- **Anti-fraud applies regardless.** "Not a security" does not mean "not regulated":
  wire-fraud, market-manipulation, pump-and-dump, and consumer-protection laws apply in
  full. glamworks will not engage in insider front-running, coordinated pumps, undisclosed
  paid promotion, or liquidity pulls.
- **Tax.** Buying, swapping, selling, and receiving airdrops/rewards are taxable events in
  many jurisdictions; airdrops/rewards are generally ordinary income at fair market value
  when received. Consult a crypto tax professional.
- **Your jurisdiction.** Token availability and legality vary by jurisdiction; you are
  solely responsible for compliance with your local laws. The tokens are not offered where
  prohibited.
